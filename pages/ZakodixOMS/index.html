ZakodixOMS - is a modern and simple preconfigured platform for building Online Monitoring System.

Solution uses advanced technology and modern approaches.

From the box ZakodixOMS works with high-quality and reliable equipment, complying with all regulations on the relevant group of devices.

Installing ZakodixOMS gives you opportunity to use years of experience of the leading developer of hardware and software industry, as well as innovative ideas of their own in the field of telematics and video streaming.

Supported features

  • Real-time trackers monitoring
  • All object movements history
  • Tracks on maps (any object, any time)
  • Reports with tracking info
  • Live video from your cameras
  • Flexible video recording schedule
  • Storing video via S3 in cloud
  • Access management
  • Billing, payments
  • Notifications management
  • Deployment in containers
  • Load management
